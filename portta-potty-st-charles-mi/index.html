Frequently Asked Questions

What is your sanitation process between rentals?

Every unit returned to our yard undergoes a rigorous multi-step sanitation process. We start by professionally pumping the holding tank, then pressure wash the entire interior and exterior with a high-grade disinfectant cleaner. We finish by restocking all supplies and conducting a final quality inspection to ensure it’s delivered in like-new condition.

What factors should I consider besides guest count when deciding on the number of units?

Event duration is a key factor; the longer the event, the more units you'll need. You should also consider whether alcohol is being served, as this typically increases restroom usage by 30-40%. Finally, a higher ratio of female to male guests may warrant additional units to reduce wait times.

My event runs into the evening. What are my options for restroom lighting?

We have several solutions! For standard or deluxe units in St. Charles, MI, we offer small, battery-powered interior LED lights that can be added to any potty rental. For a more premium experience, our VIP units and all our restroom trailer models come with built-in interior and exterior lighting.

Is a special water hookup required for the shower and restroom combo trailer?

No, our trailers are fully self-contained. They come equipped with large-capacity onboard freshwater tanks and waste holding tanks. All they require is access to a standard power source to run the water pump and lights, which we can also supply with a generator if needed.

Are there any hidden fees in your porta potty rental cost, like delivery or taxes?

We believe in 100% transparency. The quote we provide you will be a clear, itemized breakdown that includes the rental fee, a standard delivery and pickup charge, and any applicable taxes. There are no surprise fees or hidden surcharges.
